Insurance, Compliance & Legal Protection

SH Business Management Limited operates a professional consultancy model aligned with UK regulatory expectations, public sector procurement standards, and best practice governance.

Insurance Cover

  • Professional Indemnity Insurance
  • Public Liability Insurance
  • Employer’s Liability Insurance (where required)

Insurance levels are maintained appropriate to consultancy, real estate advisory, HMO compliance support, and public sector engagement across the UK and Northern Ireland.

Partner-Led Service Model

SH Business Management Limited operates a partner-led delivery model. Where specialist services fall outside our direct consultancy scope, we collaborate with vetted professional partners.

  • Partner-led accountancy services
  • Partner-led business law advisory
  • Partner-led property management agencies
  • Partner-led maintenance and compliance works
  • Partner-led insurance and brokerage services

All partner services are delivered under their own professional responsibility, regulation, and insurance arrangements.

Regulatory Position

SH Business Management Limited provides consultancy and advisory services only. We do not provide regulated legal, financial, or insurance advice.

Clients are encouraged to seek independent advice from appropriately regulated professionals where required.
