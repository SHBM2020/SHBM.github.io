Insurance & Brokerage (Partner-Led)

Insurance and brokerage services are delivered through FCA-authorised partner firms. SH Business Management Ltd does not act as an insurer or broker and does not provide regulated financial advice.

Our role is advisory and coordination-based, supporting compliant insurance arrangements aligned with public sector and corporate contract requirements.
