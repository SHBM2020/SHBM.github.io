Who We Are

S H Business Management Limited is a professional consultancy supporting businesses, landlords, investors and public-sector organisations across the UK and Northern Ireland. We provide practical, compliant and commercially focused advice across real estate, property management, HMO compliance and business operations.

With over 5 years of trading and more than 23 years of combined experience in business, real estate and community-focused work, we support clients with both strategic planning and hands-on problem solving.

  • Real estate advisory and investment appraisal
  • Landlord and portfolio support services
  • HMO setup, licensing and compliance guidance
  • Public sector and council-ready consultancy

Who We Support

We support all clients with business or real estate challenges, including:

  • UK and overseas property investors
  • SMEs and growing businesses
  • Local authorities and housing bodies
  • Public and community-led organisations
