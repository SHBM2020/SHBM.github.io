SH Business Management Ltd

SH Business Management Ltd is a UK-based advisory and consultancy firm supporting public sector bodies, housing providers, landlords, investors, and corporate clients across housing, compliance, regeneration and contract delivery.

Core Services

  • Public sector and housing advisory
  • HMO compliance and standards advisory
  • Private rented sector and landlord support
  • Business and contract readiness
  • Regeneration and community consultation

Insurance & Brokerage (Partner-Led)

Insurance and brokerage support services are delivered through FCA-authorised partner firms. SH Business Management Ltd does not act as an insurer or insurance broker.

Our Role

We operate in an advisory, coordination and procurement-support capacity only. All regulated insurance advice, placement and administration is delivered by authorised third-party partners.

What We Provide

  • Insurance requirements mapping for public sector and corporate contracts
  • Coordination with FCA-regulated insurance brokers
  • Contract-specific insurance alignment (PI, PL, EL, D&O)
  • Risk and compliance documentation support
  • Ongoing contract oversight and governance support

Who This Is For

  • Local authorities and public bodies
  • Housing providers and regeneration programmes
  • Corporate and development partners
  • Prime contractors requiring compliant insurance structures

All insurance services are delivered under separate contractual arrangements with FCA-regulated partners. SH Business Management Ltd provides no regulated financial advice.
