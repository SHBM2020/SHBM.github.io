Our Fees

SH Business Management Limited offers transparent and competitive fees for our consultancy, HMO compliance, real estate advisory, and partner-led services. All fees are structured to provide compliance and value for both UK and Northern Ireland clients.

UK Fees

  • HMO Feasibility & Licensing: £500 – £1,200 per property
  • Property Compliance Support: £350 – £950 per property
  • Real Estate Advisory & Investment Analysis: £750 – £2,500 per project
  • Business & Governance Consultancy: £500 – £1,500 per engagement
  • Partner-Led Accountancy & Legal Services: Fee on request

Overseas Fees

  • HMO Feasibility & Licensing: £600 – £1,400 per property
  • Property Compliance Support: £450 – £1,050 per property
  • Real Estate Advisory & Investment Analysis: £900 – £3,000 per project

  • Partner-Led Accountancy & Legal Services: Fee on request

Note: Fees are indicative and subject to project scope. SH Business Management Limited ensures that all services are compliant with UK and Northern Ireland regulations, fully documented, and legally protected.
