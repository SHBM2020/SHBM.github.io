Professional Fees & Engagement Structure

Our fees reflect the senior, partner-led consultancy services provided to landlords, investors, businesses, and public sector clients across the UK and internationally.

Consultancy Fee Structure

  • Initial Consultation: Complimentary (subject to scope)
  • Hourly Advisory: From £150 per hour
  • Project-Based Consultancy: Fixed fees agreed in advance
  • Retainer Services: Available for ongoing advisory support

Landlords & Property Clients

Fees are tailored based on portfolio size, complexity, and regulatory requirements. This includes HMO compliance, feasibility studies, portfolio oversight, and partner-led management agency coordination.

Investors & Commercial Clients

Investor services are priced according to deal size, risk profile, and advisory scope. This includes acquisition strategy, due diligence support, and access to partner-led accountancy, business law, insurance, and brokerage services.

Public Sector & Community Projects

Public sector and community-based projects are quoted on a proposal basis, aligned with procurement standards, compliance frameworks, and funding structures.

UK & International Engagements

  • UK-wide consultancy services
  • International advisory by arrangement
  • Remote and on-site engagement options

Transparency & Governance

All fees are confirmed in writing prior to engagement. SH Business Management Limited operates with full transparency, professional indemnity coverage, and partner-led delivery.
