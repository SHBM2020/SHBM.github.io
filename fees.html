Fees & Engagement Structure

Transparent, professional consultancy fees for landlords, investors, public sector organisations and international clients across the UK & Northern Ireland.

UK Consultancy Fees

  • Initial Consultation: £150 – £300 (fixed fee)
  • Landlord & HMO Advisory: £75 – £120 per hour or project-based from £750+
  • Investor & Real Estate Advisory: £100 – £150 per hour or project-based from £1,250+
  • Public Sector & Council Consultancy: Day rates £450 – £750 or tender-based (POA)

Worldwide / Overseas Services

  • International Consultancy: £120 – £200 per hour
  • Project-Based Overseas Work: From £2,000+

Overseas services are scoped individually to ensure compliance with local regulations and professional standards.

Partner-Led Professional Services

SH Business Management Limited works with vetted professional partners to provide enhanced service delivery.

  • Partner-led accountancy
  • Partner-led business law
  • Partner-led insurance & brokerage
  • Partner-led maintenance & repairs

Partner-led services are quoted separately or incorporated within agreed project scopes.

Engagement Terms

  • All work is subject to a written scope of services
  • Fixed fees available for defined projects
  • Retainers available for ongoing consultancy
  • VAT applied where applicable
