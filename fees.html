Our Approach to Fees

We operate a transparent and flexible fee structure aligned to market standards. All engagements begin with an initial consultation followed by a written proposal clearly outlining scope, deliverables and costs.

Indicative Fee Ranges

  • Initial Consultation: £150 – £300
  • Landlord Compliance Review: £495 – £995
  • HMO Feasibility & Setup: £1,500 – £4,500+
  • Investor Advisory & Appraisals: £750 – £2,500
  • Ongoing Advisory Retainers: £250 – £1,500 per month

Fees vary depending on complexity, location and scope of work.

Public Sector & Bespoke Engagements

Public sector, council and housing body engagements are priced on a project or retainer basis and aligned with procurement and governance requirements.

Typical public-sector engagements range from £5,000 to £50,000+, depending on scope and duration.
