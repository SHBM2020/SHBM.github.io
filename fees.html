Fees & Engagement Structure

Transparent, proportionate and flexible fee structures aligned with the scope and complexity of each engagement.

Our fees reflect the professional nature of our consultancy services and are designed to provide clarity, fairness and value. All work is confirmed in writing prior to commencement.

Engagement Models

  • Initial Consultation: Fixed-fee introductory consultation to assess requirements and scope.
  • Fixed-Fee Projects: Clearly defined services with agreed deliverables.
  • Retainer Arrangements: Ongoing advisory support for landlords, investors or organisations.
  • Public Sector Engagements: Fees aligned with procurement frameworks and project scope.

Indicative Market Rates

Final fees are confirmed following an initial discussion and depend on complexity, duration and risk profile. Indicative ranges are:

  • Initial Consultation: £150 – £300
  • Landlord & Property Advisory: £500 – £2,500 per project
  • HMO Setup & Compliance Guidance: £1,000 – £5,000 depending on scope
  • Investor Advisory: £750 – £3,500 per engagement
  • Retainer Support: From £500 per month
  • Public Sector Consultancy: Project-based or day rates aligned with sector norms

Value & Transparency

We do not charge commission, referral fees or success-based incentives. Our advice is independent, structured and focused on sustainable outcomes.
