Who We Are

S H Business Management Limited is a professional consultancy delivering real estate advisory, property management support, HMO setup guidance and business consultancy services across the UK and Northern Ireland.

We have been trading for over 5 years with more than 23 years of combined experience across business, real estate, compliance and community-focused projects.

We support all clients with any business or real estate challenges — from landlords and investors to SMEs, councils and public-sector bodies.
