Public Sector & Council Services

SH Business Management Limited provides council-ready advisory and compliance support to local authorities, housing bodies and public sector organisations across the UK and Northern Ireland.

Our services are designed to support regulatory compliance, governance, regeneration programmes and private rented sector oversight.

Public Sector Support Areas

  • HMO standards and compliance advisory
  • Private Rented Sector (PRS) support
  • Housing standards and enforcement support
  • Regeneration and community consultation advisory
  • Council-ready capability and compliance support

Governance & Compliance First

We operate independently and do not act as a managing agent. Our role is to provide objective advice, compliance insight and governance-aligned support.

Where delivery or specialist services are required, these are provided through vetted, regulated partner organisations.
