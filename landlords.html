How We Support Landlords

SH Business Management Limited provides independent advisory and compliance-led services to landlords, property owners and portfolio investors. We support both private and public-sector-aligned landlords with structured, regulation-focused guidance.

HMO Advisory & Compliance

  • HMO feasibility and suitability assessments
  • Licensing readiness and compliance guidance
  • Local authority standards and regulatory alignment
  • Operational risk and compliance reviews

Portfolio & Landlord Consultancy

  • Single property and portfolio-level advisory
  • Compliance strategy and governance support
  • Landlord readiness for council and housing association engagement
  • Independent oversight and reporting

Partner-Led Managing Agent Support

Where property management services are required, SH Business Management Limited works exclusively with vetted, independent managing agents through a partner-led model.

We are not a managing agent. Management, repairs and day-to-day operations are delivered by regulated third-party partners, ensuring transparency, accountability and full compliance with UK property regulations.

  • Investors and developers
  • Public sector and council-aligned landlords
